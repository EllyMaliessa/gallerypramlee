Awards & Recognitions

Honouring the lifetime achievements and posthumous recognitions of the legendary P. Ramlee.

Darjah Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM)

Posthumously awarded the title “Tan Sri” in 1990 by the Yang di-Pertuan Agong.

Festival Filem Malaysia

Special Jury Award in 1980 for his contribution to the film industry.

Inducted into ASEAN Hall of Fame

Recognized as one of Southeast Asia’s greatest entertainers in 2004.

Tribute in Muzium Negara

Dedicated exhibition space since 1995 celebrating his personal artifacts and legacy.

P. Ramlee Street

Major streets in Kuala Lumpur and Penang were renamed in his honour.

Google Doodle Tribute

Featured globally on Google’s homepage on his birthday, 22 March 2017.

“His legacy is the pride of a nation, and his work is immortal.”
— Malaysian Film Critics Association
